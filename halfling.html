Halfling Course Rules

18 holes played in 2 rounds, 9 holes per round, 2 winners

A front 9 winner

A back 9 winner

Starting holes 1 and 10, are "non-magical", and will be par 4

Thereafter, the winner of each hole, rolls a D6 dice to determine the par, and any "magical" effect, for the next hole to be played.

Dice score Par Magic Effect Magic Effect description
1 N/A Lucky Charm Reroll, if you reroll another 1, its an automatic 6, see below
2 par 4 Stinky Deuce Any deuces (2/6) score par at best, any other penalties apply
3 par 3 No Magical Effect N/A
4 par 4 Slay Monotony If previous hole was a par 4, then re-roll ( loop until you dont roll a 4! )
5 par 5 No Magical Effect N/A
6 par 4 Lucky Nosferatu Your score for the hole improves by 1, and everyone elses score worsens by 1

Rolling When Hole Tied

Where a hole is tied between players, all those players roll for the next hole, an average is taken ( after all and any re-rolls ), to determine the par ( rounded down ). The magical effects of each players individual roll stands.

When 1/6 and X/6 scored

Any player scoring 1/6 or X/6 has the effect of dispelling any magical effects in play for that hole. All X/6 each carry an additional penalty point to all players scores for that hole. All 1/6 each carry an additional bonus point to all players scores for that hole.
