Halfling Course Rules

18 holes played in 2 rounds, 9 holes per round, 2 winners

A front 9 winner

A back 9 winner

Starting holes 1 and 10, are "non-magical", and will be par 4

Thereafter, the winner of each hole, rolls a D6 dice to determine the par, and any "magical" effect, for the next hole to be played.
